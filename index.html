ABOUT

In front of me, ever peacefully, floating in the moonlight..

Currently based in Honolulu, Seiichi is an independent singer, songwriter, composer, producer, and multi-instrumentalist. Seiichi holds an Associates of Fine Arts in Music from Harper College, where he learned theory, composition, piano, and jazz and classical guitar. As an exemplary student, he was awarded the Excellence in the Discipline Award for Music.

His award was attributed to his thorough understanding of music theory, his role as a theory tutor, the hundreds of hours spent in the piano room composing classical pieces for extra credit, his indie/folk recordings, jazz guitar gigs, and his jazz album “Coldest Evening of the Year”.

Seiichi is now focusing on his solo singer/songwriter act and is compiling an album titled “hōchi”. A sorrowful and deeply piercing expression of love, written about the process of separation with his wife.
